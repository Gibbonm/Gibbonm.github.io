Meal Plans For Runners


If you’re taking on a challenge this year, be it a 5k or a marathon, getting your training diet spot on will help you go that extra mile. There are several key elements of nutrition it’s important to consider to maximise performance. For endurance training, your calorie intake should reflect the total volume of training, which is known as ‘periodising’ the training diet. Your plan should typically be divided into three, depending on the volume of training on that day.
